 1. Organisation Overview

Organisation Name: ZIQHENYE FOUNDATION

Brief History
ZIQHENYE FOUNDATION is a non-profit organisation established in 2022 with the purpose of reducing period poverty and supporting women and girls who struggle to access menstrual products. The organisation focuses on community development through education, sanitary product distribution, outreach programmes, and empowerment initiatives.

The foundation was created to ensure that women and girls have access to essential products and opportunities without being disadvantaged by financial limitations.

 Mission Statement
To provide free sanitary products, education, support, and empowerment opportunities for women and girls.

 Vision Statement
To create a future where no girl misses opportunities because of period poverty.

 Target Audience
• Women and girls in vulnerable communities
• School learners
• Sponsors and donors
• Volunteers
• Schools and community organisations
• Community leaders
