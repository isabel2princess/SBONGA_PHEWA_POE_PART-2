Our Support Services

We provide support, education, and essential resources to empower women and girls and fight period poverty within communities.

Free Sanitary Pad Distribution

We provide free sanitary products to girls and women experiencing financial difficulties. Our goal is to ensure dignity, confidence, and equal opportunities for all.

Community Outreach Programmes

We regularly visit schools and communities to distribute products, provide support, and connect with vulnerable groups.

Menstrual Health Education

Our foundation promotes awareness and education around menstrual health, hygiene, and breaking stigma within communities.

Volunteer & Sponsorship Opportunities

We welcome volunteers, sponsors, and donors who want to contribute towards helping women and girls access essential resources.

Creating Community Impact

Every donation, volunteer, sponsor, and supporter contributes to changing lives and creating opportunities for women and girls.
