About ZIQHENYE FOUNDATION

ZIQHENYE FOUNDATION is a non-profit organisation established in 2022 with the goal of empowering women and girls through access to sanitary products, education, and community support.

Our foundation believes that dignity, education, health, and equal opportunities should be accessible to every woman and girl regardless of financial circumstances.

Our Story

ZIQHENYE FOUNDATION was created to address period poverty and support girls who struggle to access essential menstrual products.

Through donations, community partnerships, outreach programmes, and volunteers, we aim to create lasting positive change within communities.

Our Mission

To provide free sanitary products, support, education, and opportunities that empower women and girls.

Our Vision

A future where no girl misses education or opportunities because of period poverty.

Our Values

Empowerment, Integrity, Respect, Community, Support, and Dignity.

What We Do

Our foundation focuses on community upliftment through practical support and awareness programmes.

Free Pad Distribution

Providing sanitary products to schools, communities, and vulnerable groups.

Menstrual Education

Creating awareness around menstrual hygiene and health education.

Community Outreach

Supporting women and girls through outreach programmes and events.

Empowerment

Helping girls build confidence, dignity, and self-worth.

Our Impact

Every product distributed, every volunteer, and every donation contributes towards creating stronger communities and better opportunities.
