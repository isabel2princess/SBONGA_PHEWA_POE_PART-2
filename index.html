Supporting Women & Girls Through Dignity and Care

ZIQHENYE FOUNDATION is dedicated to fighting period poverty by providing free sanitary products, education, and community support to women and girls.

Explore Our Services

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that no girl misses school, opportunities, or confidence because she lacks access to menstrual products.

We believe dignity, education, and health should be accessible to every woman and girl.

Free Pad Distribution

Providing sanitary products to schools, communities, and vulnerable groups.

Education Programmes

Promoting menstrual health awareness and breaking harmful stigma.

Community Outreach

Supporting women and girls through outreach initiatives and community engagement.

Creating Positive Change

Since 2022, our foundation has focused on empowering communities by supporting girls and women with dignity, education, and essential resources.
